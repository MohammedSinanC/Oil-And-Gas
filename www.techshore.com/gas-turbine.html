TECHSHORE CTSC PRODUCTION OPERATION COURSE

Discipline: - Production Operations (For Petroleum / Process Engineers & Technicians)
Course Title: - TP 29 Gas Turbine Operations

Course Objectives: -

  To provide a basic knowledge of the theory of design and principle of operation of gas turbines used in Oil & Gas production facilities.
  To encourage correct, safe and efficient responsive action to gas turbine operational problems.
  To encourage correct routine checks for trouble free running and longer machine life.

Target Audience: - Production / Process Engineers/ Technicians/ Supervisors

Contact: - E mail ID :- techshoreinspections@gmail.com
Phones: 0484 4050333, 9946399970
Course Location : On request Course Dates: - On request Course Duration: - 3 days ( 5 hrs per day)
Course Fee : On request (Depending upon number of candidates registered)
Requirement: Existing working candidates in Petrochemical Production operations Instructor : - Joy Paul, Graduate with 44 years field training experience with A1 & V1 (Edexcel)certification in Oil & Gas production / operation around the world (ARAMCO, SHELL, ADMA-OPCO, PDO).

Contents

  Introduction

  • Basic gas turbine, Single shaft & two shaft turbines
  Pressure and temperature of a gas turbine cycle
  Gas turbine components
  • Compressor, Turbine, Combustion chamber
  • Fuel gas system
  Auxiliaries of the combustion gas turbine
  • Starter, Governor, Over speed trip mechanism
  • Seals, Bearings , Lube oil
  Operation controls
  • Temperature control, Vibration, Start-up and normal running
  Turbine operation
  • Start sequence, Daily checks , Trouble shooting , Safety precautions
  "Hands on" techniques
  Pre and post course assessment
